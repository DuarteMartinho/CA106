Football Teams

Here we will look at 3 types of teams; Professional, Semi Pro and Amateur.



Professional level football is extremely competitive with huge revenue being brought in and extremely large viewships with players being paid millions yearly to compete at this highest level

At this level there is well known teams such as Real Madrid, Liverpool and Bayern Munich, with world renown players such as Cristiano Ronaldo and Lionel Messi.



Semi professional level football ivolves players who are payed sums to play but not enough for them to play full time and often have side jobs but make a large commitment to the team with tickets being sold for viewers in the local area.

At this level many of the players work seperate side jobs and even though they are not payed lots they still have a passion for the game and wish to play at a competitive level because they find it very enjoyable.


At amateur level the players are not payed and often pay the clubs they play for so they can play so referee fees and pitch maintenace but are happy to pay for the love of the game.

Players at this level are very passionate and often only train weekly or not at all and play for the enjoyment, excersize and social aspect of the game.
