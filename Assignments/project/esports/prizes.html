Prizes

Winners

Prizes from the eSports tournaments are awarded on a position based system. Depending on the tournament some competitions award the top 3 teams with money and the winning team with both the money and a trophy.

The eSports cup

The eSports trophy is iconic as it is commonly used as the logo for esea. The eSports trophy cup is awarded to the winning team of the tournament, it is one of the biggest achievements one can strive for as a player on a professional level.

Money prizes

The prize money is often determined by the popularity of the game, the top 5 games have prize pools from 1million - 15million USD. If we take this into account then the team in 1st place could receive 700,000 USD, second place earns 250,000 USD and 3rd with 50,000 USD as an example.
