Competitions

What happens during a competition?

The typical order of a competition starts off with the checking in with tickets through gates to enter the stadium. Once the spectators enter the stadium they can find many stalls similar to a convention with game related items and announcements of future game releases. Here fans can also sometimes meet the professional players and get autographs from them.

The arena

Once the time for the scheduled tournament arrives, spectators are seated in their assigned places and can watch the professional matches in person which makes the emotions and experience more immersive similar to that of a movie theatre or a concert.

Professional players

The job of the professional players includes spending a lot of time training and getting prepared for their tournaments. It is also important not to overdo the training as this can cause problems with being burnt out. If players have spare time they can go and do fan meets and sign autographs which creates a positive community and fanbase.
