Top 10 most viewed eSports Games

Here is a top 10 list of the most viewed eSport games. This data is taken from the viewer count and viewing duration from streaming online services that eSports posts to. These streams, including the event in the stadium, are always sponsored and this helps create revenue for eSports to host future events.

Counter-Strike

Counter-Strike is one among many games that are actively being played professionally. Counter-Strike is a great example of an online video game that requires a lot of time and dedication to master. This game has many different game modes, including, casual, for casual play and competitive to name a few. The competitive game modes are ranked and as you play more with good consistency you can level up to higher ranks. This makes the game more difficult as you play. The game requires a team of 5 players and a good skill in aiming, which can be perfected with practice. As a result of this Counter-Strike became one of the most popular first person shooting games to date that has yearly tournaments with a massive fan base of over 20 million people and 720,000 concurrent players.

Steam activity statistics

Steam is one of the greatest game services that exist online today. It is a service where you can purchase games, upload your games/addons, interact with others, sell and trade virtual items. In the year of 2018 Steam has made a net worth of over 8 billion USD from these transactions alone. Steam has over 1 billion registered users and a concurrent player base of 20 million in the year of 2020.
