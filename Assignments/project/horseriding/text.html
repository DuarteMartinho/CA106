Horse riding is one of the most enjoyable activities, it is suitable for yound and old, novices or experts. There are excellent training facilities throughout Ireland where you can learn to ride, for more experienced riders there are many riding clubs or even Interschools leagues.

How to get started?

If you are looking to get started in horse riding you need to follow the following steps:

  1. First you need to find a riding school. The standards are set by the Association Of Riding Establishments (AIRE), which promotes safety for everyone.
  2. You may then book a lesson, you may need to take into account if you need to buy any gear and wonder how much money it will cost.
  3. Should take into account travel time and see if there are any options closer to you.
  4. Don't forget to have FUN!
