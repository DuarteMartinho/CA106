On this page we will take a look at some highlights from the sport to give you a greater understanding of how the game is played.



Here on showcase are some of footballs most spectacular goals that vary in requiring amazing technical skill and having huge impact in the games that they were scored.

Goals are how you win games and this makes them extremely important, this is why strikers are at the top of admiration in football as these are the ones scoring the majority of goals.




Football is an 11v11 sport and requires a huge amount of teamwork for the team to perform effectively and win games.

Teamwork is fundamentaly an easy task but for a team to work together to perform at the highest level it takes many hours of practice and good coaching.




Here is a display of many of the great saves done by goalkeepers to stop the other team scoring a goal, These saves take a huge deal of athletitiscm and cat like reflexes to pull off and require lots of training and experience.

Goalkeeepers a vital part of every team but are still at times underappriciated for their contribution to the team.
